Installing Zoom
Scroll down for step by step instructions.

Step 1 : Go to zoom.com/download and click download.


Step 2 : Click keep if prompted by your browser


Step 3 : Click the Zoom installer


Step 4 : Zoom will now install, once it is done it will let you know it has been installed.


Step 5 : Press done, Zoom is now installed.

Using Zoom
Scroll down for step by step instructions.

Step 1 : Get the zoom meeting details from a friend


Step 2 : Open Zoom and press join meeting


Step 3 : Enter the meeting id


Step 4 : Enter your name.


Step 5 : Press the join button to join the meeting.


Step 6 : Press join with audio to enable audio.


Note : Presssing the link will also work. You will simply have to accept the dialog box.
